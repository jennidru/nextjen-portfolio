Front-End Web Developer & UX Generalist.

I'm Jen Hurst, & no day is complete without coffee and good dose of code.

It hasn’t always been this way. I started my career by earning a BS in Biochemistry and doing nearly a decade of cutting-edge “Next Gen” biomedical research in Silicon Valley. When I started to code for jobs and eventually build websites from the ground up, I quickly found that my trained analytical mind gave me an edge for problem-solving in programming, too. I also realized I found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work.


Now I've made a full switch to the "NextJen" of Jen - front-end web development with a deep focus on user-centered design. How many times have you pushed a door that you were supposed to pull? That’s an example of user experience at work, and unfortunately, it’s often overlooked in website and app design. Small irritants like this can add up online, and cost your business customers and repeat visits.

But that doesn’t happen here. I use my unique research skills, UX experience, and eye for detail to write clean, elegant, responsive code that delights users every time.
